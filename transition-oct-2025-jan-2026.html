How nature of change is determined. For each requirement present in both releases, we compare the normalised wording of the previous and current versions and measure how much of it changed. We then classify the result into four categories:
  • identical — no textual difference.
  • editorial — very small wording changes, typically formatting or minor rephrasing.
  • clarification — meaningful rewording without changing intent.
  • substantive — material change to what the requirement asks for.
These categories drive the Nature column in the tables below.
